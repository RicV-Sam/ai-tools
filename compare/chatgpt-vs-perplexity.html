ChatGPT vs Perplexity

ChatGPT and Perplexity solve different problems. ChatGPT is the broader assistant for drafting, planning, and flexible conversations. Perplexity is the more research-first choice when source visibility and current information matter most.

Updated regularly

Last reviewed: April 2026

Quick winner summary

Choose ChatGPT for broad assistant value across mixed tasks. Choose Perplexity when you want research speed, references, and an answer engine built around current information.

Who should choose which

Choose ChatGPT if you want one flexible assistant for writing, brainstorming, study help, and general work support. Choose Perplexity if your biggest question is finding and checking information quickly rather than chatting through every task.

Final verdict

ChatGPT is the better everyday generalist. Perplexity is the better research-first specialist. For many people, the best answer is using Perplexity to gather and ChatGPT to reshape and draft.
