FAQs

What is the best all-round AI productivity tool?

ChatGPT is still one of the easiest broad starting points for mixed everyday tasks.

Which AI productivity tool is better for Google users?

Gemini makes the most sense if Gmail, Docs, Drive, and Google-centric workflows matter to you.

Which tool is best for research-heavy work?

Perplexity is often the better fit when current information and cited answers matter more than conversational breadth.
