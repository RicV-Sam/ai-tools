FAQs

What is the best AI research tool for current information?

Perplexity is usually the strongest first stop when source visibility and freshness matter most.

Can general assistants still help with research?

Yes. ChatGPT and Gemini are often useful for summarising, organising, and reframing information after the initial gathering stage.

Should I trust AI research tools without checking sources?

No. Even research-first tools should be used with source checking and human judgment.
