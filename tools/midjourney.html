Midjourney

Midjourney is one of the most recognisable AI image tools for stylised visual output, concept art, moodboards, and creative exploration. It is usually considered when image quality and aesthetic output matter more than workflow breadth.

Updated regularly

Last reviewed: April 2026

Overview

Midjourney is best known for strong visual style and a creator-first feel. It is less about broad productivity and more about producing striking images quickly for ideation, design direction, and creative iteration.

Best for

  • Designers and creators generating concept visuals
  • Stylised artwork, moodboards, and visual direction
  • People who care most about image quality and aesthetics
  • Creators comparing Midjourney with more workflow-broad tools like Runway

Strengths

  • Very strong visual style and creative output
  • Well suited to concept exploration and aesthetics-heavy work
  • Recognisable place in creator tool comparisons
  • Clear fit inside image-first workflows

Weaknesses

  • Less useful if you need video or broader creator workflows too
  • No long-term free tier in the current plan structure
  • Not the best single-tool choice for cross-media production

Pricing snapshot

Midjourney's official plans currently include Basic at $10/month, Standard at $30/month, Pro at $60/month, and Mega at $120/month, with lower effective monthly pricing on annual billing.

Free plan info

Midjourney is mainly a paid product. That means it is harder to test casually than tools with an always-available free plan.

Is it worth it?

Midjourney is worth it when image quality and visual style are the main priority. If you need a creator tool that spans video and broader production work, compare Runway before subscribing.
