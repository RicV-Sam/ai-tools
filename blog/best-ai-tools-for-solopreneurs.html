Best AI Tools for Solopreneurs

Solopreneurs need AI tools that multiply their time without creating an unmanageable stack. In practice that usually means one broad assistant, one research tool, and only a small number of specialists.

Updated regularly

Last reviewed: April 2026

Strong starting stack

ChatGPT is the broad all-rounder. Perplexity helps with research. Claude may be the better writing add-on if tone and long-form output matter. Cursor is relevant for builder-solopreneurs, while Runway and ElevenLabs matter more for creator-led businesses.

How to keep the stack small

Start with the tool you will use every day. Add only one specialist after a repeated bottleneck becomes obvious. That usually leads to better value than collecting overlapping subscriptions.
