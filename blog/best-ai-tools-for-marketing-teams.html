Best AI Tools for Marketing Teams

Marketing teams need tools that help with writing, research, visuals, and production without creating more workflow friction. The strongest stack is usually small, practical, and tied to recurring campaign work.

Updated regularly

Last reviewed: April 2026

Best options to start with

Claude is strong for campaign writing and messaging drafts. ChatGPT is useful as a broad all-round assistant. Perplexity helps with research and competitor checks. Runway and ElevenLabs matter once the team adds visual and audio production to the stack.

What matters most

For many teams, the right question is not which tool is best overall. It is which tool helps the current workflow the most: copy, research, creative production, or campaign iteration.
