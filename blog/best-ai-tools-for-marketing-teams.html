Best AI Tools for Marketing Teams

Marketing teams do not need generic AI hype. They need tools that speed up strategy, research, drafting, production, and iteration without creating more review chaos. This page is built around that workflow reality.

Updated for April 2026

Best overall for most marketing teams: ChatGPT. Best research add-on: Perplexity.

The most useful picks

Best overall

ChatGPT

Best for briefs, messaging drafts, planning, and day-to-day marketing work.

Best for research

Perplexity

Best for market scans, topic discovery, and source-backed competitor checks.

Best for higher-quality copy

Claude

Best when the team cares more about cleaner prose and long-form drafts.

Best for creators

Runway

Best if the team is also responsible for AI-assisted visual production.

Who should use what?

Use ChatGPT if your team needs one default assistant. Use Perplexity if research accuracy and speed are pain points. Use Claude if your team writes a lot of high-stakes copy. Use Runway if AI is moving into campaign production and creative assets, not just text.

What most marketing teams should avoid

A fragmented stack full of overlapping tools. In most cases, one broad assistant plus one specialist is enough. The mistake is buying three tools that all kind of do the same thing.

Best first paid tool for marketing teams

ChatGPT is usually the best first paid tool because it covers the widest range of team use cases. Perplexity is the best second paid tool if research is dragging the team down.
