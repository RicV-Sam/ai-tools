FAQs

What is the best first AI tool for students?

ChatGPT is still the broadest and easiest first choice for most students.

Should students pay for AI tools right away?

Usually no. Most students should test free access first and only pay when usage becomes regular enough to justify it.

Which tool is best for research-heavy coursework?

Perplexity is often the best addition once source-backed research becomes a bigger need.
