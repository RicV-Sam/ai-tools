FAQs

What is the best first AI tool for a small business?

ChatGPT is still the broadest and simplest starting point for many small teams.

Do small businesses need multiple AI tools?

Usually not at first. Most should start with one broad assistant, then add specialists only when the need is clear.

When is a specialist worth paying for?

Specialists make sense once a repeated workflow such as research, content creation, or coding justifies the extra cost.
