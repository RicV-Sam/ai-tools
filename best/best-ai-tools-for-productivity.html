FAQs

What is the best overall AI productivity tool?

ChatGPT remains the broadest first choice for many users because it handles the widest range of mixed tasks well.

When should I choose Gemini instead?

Gemini makes more sense if Google apps are already central to your work and that ecosystem fit matters.

Do productivity users need a research-first tool too?

Sometimes. Perplexity is often worth adding once information gathering becomes a daily bottleneck.
